OUR STORY

Building Dreams Since 2004

RC Services started with a simple mission: to provide South Florida with construction services that prioritize quality, integrity, and customer satisfaction. What began as a small family operation has grown into one of the region's most trusted construction companies.

Over the past two decades, we've completed over 500 projects ranging from luxury custom homes to commercial developments. Our commitment to excellence has earned us a 98% customer satisfaction rate and countless referrals from happy clients.
